Our Staff

Discover the passionate educators who shape the future of our students.

Gloria Freeman-Fromayan

Founding Member / Principal

No Child is left behind | principal@lisabj.org

Gloria Freeman-Fromayan is a professional administrator and educator with over 30 years of experience in American-style education. She holds a certificate in International School Leadership, a Master’s Degree in Education, and a Bachelor’s Degree in Elementary Education. Her long and productive career in teaching and administration has enriched her ability to foster a nurturing learning environment.

Founder Member Luke International School

Samilia Afrifa

Head Teacher & Teacher of Grades 1 and 2

Samilia Afrifa is the Head Teacher responsible for motivating students to enjoy learning. She uses her experience to make a significant difference in their lives. Luke International School offers her the opportunity to bring her ambition to life in a purely Christian setting—combining knowledge with the right values from early childhood development.

Renee Rutta

Renee Rutta is very excited to be a part of Luke International School. Her passion lies in pouring the love of God into her students and using her extensive experience with the A Beka program to develop strong character and academic foundations.

Elvina Henries

Elvina Henries loves seeing children progress throughout the year. Watching her students learn, from tying a shoelace to reading a new word, is the most rewarding aspect of her job.

Liliane Owayo
