Our Board

Meet the visionary founders who created Luke International School

LEADERSHIP

Visionary Leadership

Our board members bring diverse expertise and a shared commitment to educational excellence, guiding our school with wisdom and foresight.

Gloria Freeman-Fromayan

Directrice / Founding Member / Principal

Gloria Freeman-Fromayan is a professional administrator and educator with over thirty years of experience in American style education. With certifications in International School Leadership, a Master's in Education and a Bachelor's in Elementary Education, she has dedicated her career to developing young minds and ensuring smooth school administration.

Rosalind Brown

Founding Member

Rosalind Brown, a founding member, holds a Master of Arts in International Education and a Bachelor's in Economics and Finance. Her extensive international experience, including work with the United Nations and accreditation visits in Africa, reflects her commitment to global unity and educational excellence.

Nathalie Akon Gabala

Founding Member

Nathalie Akon Gabala is a Partner in charge of African Investments with a decade of expertise at Standard Chartered Bank. With advanced degrees in management, finance and accounting, she serves on various financial boards and co-founded an NGO dedicated to supporting sick children. Her business acumen enriches the school's foundation.

Martha Rahman

Founding Member

Martha Rahman, with a Bachelor's in Biblical Studies and a Master's in Christian Education, has dedicated 18 years to teaching. As founder of two elementary schools and a certified educational leader, she champions early child reading and strategic leadership in education.

Abeni Jackson-Johnson

Founding Member

Abeni Jackson-Johnson brings diverse cultural insights with her background in US education and special education. With experience from Flint to Arlington and a portfolio that includes international teaching and outreach, she plays a key role in shaping a supportive, innovative educational environment.

OUR APPROACH

Leadership Philosophy

Our board members are united by a shared commitment to educational excellence and nurturing the leaders of tomorrow.

Guiding Principles

Our board members bring a wealth of expertise from education, business, and non-profit sectors to guide Luke International School's development. This diverse leadership team ensures that our school benefits from multiple perspectives, creating a rich educational environment for our students.

United by their commitment to educational excellence, the board works together to establish policies, oversee financial stability, and ensure the school fulfills its mission to provide a world-class education within a nurturing Christian international environment.
