ACADEMICS OVERVIEW

Luke International School (LIS) uses the accredited ABEKA CURRICULUM. The “ABEKA” program is a comprehensive, quality curriculum written from a Christian perspective.

The program was founded by Dr. Arlin and Beka Horton over 40 years ago. The goal of the “ABEKA” program is to continually improve and provide the most up-to-date content and best practices in textbooks and curriculum.

The “ABEKA” is an accredited program that has historically met or exceeded recommended content standards, meeting almost all common core content standards (national education standards).

The “ABEKA” program carefully supports every grade level from Pre‑Nursery to 6th Grade, ensuring a consistent, high‑quality experience for every child.
