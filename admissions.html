Admissions Welcome

Begin your journey with Luke International School

DEAR PROSPECTIVE FAMILIES

On behalf of the Luke International community (LIS), welcome! I am delighted that you have chosen to explore the possibility of a LIS education for your child. LIS offers a progressive, innovative education grounded in deep tradition, preparing children with the necessary skills and attributes to succeed in today’s globally diverse world.

The educational program chosen by LIS is based on a strong American curriculum and provides intense French and English classes. Children will be exposed to cutting-edge technology and excellent instruction.

Our dedicated faculty helps each child discover their passions, nourishing every student’s mind and character while fostering courage, creativity, compassion, and strong ethics.

I invite you to experience LIS for yourself—families who visit fall in love. Attend an Open House or schedule a tour. The moment you walk through our doors, you'll know what sets LIS apart.

Please call the Admissions Office to begin the process. We look forward to meeting you!

WHO WILL BENEFIT FROM LIS?

  • Parents who want their children to follow a strong and accredited American curriculum.
  • Parents who appreciate a wholesome education with high academic and moral standards.
  • Parents who want their children to be completely bilingual, English and French.
  • Parents who value an education that is flexible and adaptable to a child’s capabilities, with a small size classroom.
