WELCOME TO LIS

Your Gateway to Excellence

Thank you for your interest in Luke International School. We offer a warm environment and a wholesome education with high academic and moral standards. We welcome new families and students with open arms and a friendly approach.

1

Admissions Process

All forms must be filled out and returned to the School along with any required payments. LIS has a rolling admission policy.

2

Assessment & Testing

Children will undergo language and placement testing to determine their academic levels and English/French language skills. We want to ensure each child's educational needs are met.

3

Welcome & Integration

Once accepted, we ensure a smooth transition with orientation sessions and personalized support to help your child feel at home in our community.
