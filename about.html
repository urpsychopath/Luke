About Luke International School

Inspiring excellence in education within a Christian international environment

OUR FOUNDATION

Vision & Mission

Our guiding principles that inspire and direct everything we do at Luke International School.

Vision Statement

We are an internationally-minded community collaborating to build global citizens and dynamic leaders with Christian values while maintaining academic excellence and ensuring that no child is left behind.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to Inspire, Educate & Enlighten our students through an outstanding education in a safe and caring Christian international environment, inspiring all our students to reach their true potential and to act as responsible participants in a global society.

OUR COMMUNITY

School Community

At LIS, our dedicated faculty and staff work together to create a nurturing and inspiring learning environment for every student.

Dedicated to Excellence

Our teaching staff consists of highly qualified educators from diverse backgrounds who are committed to providing the highest quality education. With a low student-to-teacher ratio, we ensure that each student receives the individual attention they need to thrive.

At Luke International School, we believe that a strong community is the foundation of a successful educational experience. Our staff, students, and parents work together to create a supportive and inclusive environment where everyone feels valued and respected.

LEADERSHIP

Principal's Message

A personal welcome from our school principal, Gloria Freeman-Fromayan.

Gloria Freeman-Fromayan

School Principal

We are happy to welcome each of you to the Luke International School family. It is important that as the lifelong journey of learning begins, the proper values and academic attributes are instilled. Our staff is delighted to have the opportunity to ensure our students gain a solid foundation as they begin their journey at LIS. We have worked hard to create a safe atmosphere that promotes learning in a positive way.

Luke International School understands the importance of both the English and French languages in our world. Hence we have created a program that uses the "A Beka" curriculum, which conveys our core values of academic excellence from a Christian perspective, coupled with a strong French language program. We will incorporate teaching methods that enhance the child's capacity to learn.

We want parents to see us as partners in the success of our students. It will take all our involvement to ensure our children reach their full potential. We have set up volunteering opportunities for parents both in and out of the classroom, and we keep you informed through our newsletter, progress reports, and regular PTA meetings.

– Gloria Freeman
